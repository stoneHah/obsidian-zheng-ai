当看大别人做的图片很惊艳的时候，但是又不知道提示词该怎么写，这时就可以通过反问GPT的方式，给出如何进行提问。

就拿上面这张图来说，觉得这种手绘风格+图片+标记的方式很对我的口味，但是我又不知道怎么想GPT描述我的需求时，就可以使用反推的方法。

**下面是与GPT的一个完整对话内容**

```text
## ChatGPT

要生成图片中的风格，我该怎么进行提问？

---

## ChatGPT

要生成如你上传的这张图那种风格（手写风格的笔记搭配剪贴图像和手绘图案）的图片，你可以这样描述你的需求：

---

### ✅ 提问模板（适用于AI绘图工具，比如DALL·E、Midjourney、Stable Diffusion等）：

> **Prompt（英文）**:  
> A hand-drawn style infographic page, resembling a student's notebook. The page contains blue handwritten text with red pen highlights and underlines, simple marker illustrations (e.g., hats, spiral staircases, magnifying glass), and photo cutouts of characters from a film. The topic is Alfred Hitchcock's *Vertigo*. Layout is like a school note: numbered sections, simple sketches, and sticky-note-like summaries. The handwriting is neat, with small illustrations and arrows connecting ideas.

---

### 🧠 提问时可强调的关键词：

- *Handwritten notes*
- *Notebook style*
- *Red and blue ink*
- *Marker drawings*
- *Film analysis or school project*
- *Photo cutouts + sketch combinations*
- *Infographic with visuals*

---

### 🧑🎨 如果你想换内容主题（例如介绍另一部电影、历史人物、科学概念等）：

你只需要把 prompt 中的 “topic is Alfred Hitchcock's *Vertigo* ” 替换成你想介绍的内容即可，例如：

> The topic is Marie Curie's scientific discoveries.

或者：

> The topic is the movie *Inception* by Christopher Nolan.

---

如果你告诉我你想要介绍什么内容，我可以帮你写一个完整的 prompt。你想继续吗？

---

## ChatGPT

肖申克救赎这不电影

## ChatGPT

---

### 🎨 Prompt:

> A hand-drawn style infographic page that looks like a student's notebook. The topic is the movie *The Shawshank Redemption*. The page has blue handwritten text with red pen highlights and underlines, mimicking school notes. It includes:
> 
> - A brief plot summary of *The Shawshank Redemption*
> - Key themes like hope, freedom, friendship, and perseverance
> - Simple marker-style doodles, such as prison bars, a rock hammer, a bird, and a tunnel
> - Photo cutouts of main characters (Andy Dufresne, Red, the Warden) placed alongside notes
> - Arrows and spirals connecting ideas
> - Important keywords like “hope”, “freedom”, and “justice” circled in red Layout is informal but neat, resembling a high school or college literature assignment.

---
```
